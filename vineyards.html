Vineyards

Best of New England

Although New England may not be known for our vineyards and wineries; our region is far more developed than you may have expected. Here are some of my favorite vineyards!

Labelle Winery

Labelle Winery is located in Amherst, NH. Labelle has 3 acres of planted grapes, and 11 acres of total land. The vineyards contain seven varieties of grapes (Seyval, Brianna, Petite Pearl, Petite Amie, Chancellor, Noiret, and Cayuga). In addition, Labelle has event rooms, a bistro, a tasting room, a wine cellar, a gift shop, dance classes, and much more!

Millbrook Vineyards and Winery

Millbrook Vineyards and Winery is located in Millbrook, NY. Millbrook's 30 acres of vines include plantings of Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, Cabernet Franc, and an Italian grape varietal called Tocai Friulano. This winery has tours, art competitions, a tasting room, a restaurant, and many trails for exploring the vineyards!
